Trading Tutorial

Work your way up from a simple, low-value item all the way up to valuables from kings and artists.

The Merchant-Master will give you a request and a starting item, you must attempt to connect these while trying to make some extra coin along the way.

Try your best to use the clues to make connections. On a missed trade they will reveal to you something they desire.

You may also sell items to the merchant-master by selecting the item and interacting with his box. Remember, he drives a hard bargain and will give you 1 less gold than it's market value.

Be savvy with your trades, not all trades may benefit you!
